I'm a first-year PhD student at Linköping University in the Communications for Networked Intelligent Systems Group (CNIS), supervised by Prof. Nikolaos Pappas and co-supervised by Prof. Osvaldo Simeone (with King's College London). My name Jiping (继平) in Chinese means "stay healthy and lucky". I hope my name can bring you a happier life!

I received the B.Sc. degree in electronics and information engineering from the Dalian Maritime University, Dalian, China, in 2020, and the M.Sc. degree in information and communication engineering from the Harbin Institute of Technology (HIT), Shenzhen, China, in 2023. I was a research assistant at HIT and a visiting student at Tsinghua University in 2023. Many thanks to Prof. Tingting Zhang for supporting me during my gap year.

Research Interests

My current research is about semantic communications and networked control systems, with a particular focus on defining the value (semantics) of information to reduce the amount of less important data generated, transmitted, and processed in sensor networks.
