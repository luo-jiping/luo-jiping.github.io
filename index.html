Hello!

I'm a first-year PhD student at Linköping University in the Communications for Networked Intelligent Systems Group (CNIS), advised by Prof. Nikolaos Pappas. My name (继平) in Chinese means `stay healthy and lucky'. I hope my name can bring you a happier life!

I received the B.Sc. degree in electronics and information engineering from the Dalian Maritime University, Dalian, China, in 2020, and the M.Sc. degree in information and communication engineering from the Harbin Institute of Technology (HIT), Shenzhen, China, in 2023. I was a research assistant at HIT and a visiting student at Tsinghua University in 2023. Many thanks to Prof. Tingting Zhang for supporting me during my gap year.

I am working on semantic communications, networked control systems, stochastic optimization, and reinforcement learning. I am especially interested in exploiting the value (semantics) of information in the interdisciplinary area of communication and control. My goal is to propose semantics-aware and goal-oriented communication protocols for networked control systems to reduce the amount of inefficient data transmissions.
